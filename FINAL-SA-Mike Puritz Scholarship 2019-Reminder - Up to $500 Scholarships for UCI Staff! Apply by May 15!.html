Up to $500 Scholarships for UCI Staff
Apply by 11:59 pm on May 15, 2019
For classes completed between July 1, 2019 and June 30, 2020
The Staff Assembly Mike Puritz Career Enhancement Scholarship Program is funded by donations and fundraising events. It helps UC Irvine career staff employees to pursue a variety of educational opportunities to improve their skills and obtain new ones towards a successful career at UCI.

This scholarship opportunity is open to full-time or part-time UC Irvine career staff employees who have completed their six-month probationary period. Eligible courses include those that directly or indirectly impact an employee's current or future position at UC Irvine, but are not a requirement for their current position.

Please see website for complete details: https://staffassembly.uci.edu/scholarship-program.
